Darjeeling conjures visions of snow peaks, serenity of vibrant green hills steeped in splendour, a land of breathtaking beauty crowned by the majestic Himalayas.

Gangtok is a city, municipality, the capital and the largest populated place of the Indian state of Sikkim.

MUSSORIE

Established in the Himalaya foothills by a British Army officer in 1820, the "Queen of the Hills" stands above the rest with its favorable climate and Doon Valley views.

SHILLONG

A beautiful city encircled by pine trees, Shillong is the capital of Meghalaya. It derives its name from Lei Shyllong, an idol worshipped at the Shillong Peak.

Gulmarg is the crowning glory of Jammu & Kashmir and is located 2,650 meters above sea level.
