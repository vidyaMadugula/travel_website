Calangute Beach

Popular as the “Queen of Beaches”, Calangute Beach is one of the most popular beaches not just in Goa but throughout the country. This mesmerizing beach sees lakhs of tourists every year. The place is extremely crowded especially during the peak seasons. Spread across 7 km of glittering sand, this beach captivates everyone with its incomparable charm. At a distance of 15 km from Panaji, it is easily accessible. If stories are to be believed, this beach was found by hippies during 1960s. Since then it has gained popularity not just amongst foreign but national tourists as well.




Location

Calangute is a town in the North Goa district of the Indian state of Goa. It is famous for its beach, the largest in North Goa and a popular tourist destination. The peak tourist season is during Christmas and New Year, and during the summer in May.
