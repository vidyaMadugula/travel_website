Bhagsu

Because of the waterfall and close proximity to McLeod Ganj, Dharamkot and the Triund Trek, Bhagsu has become a popular place for tourists to stay. In early 18th century, the Gorkhas came here with British to settle, and they formed the historic 1st Gorkha Rifles in 1815. Bhagsu is also the home of 1st Gorkha Rifles.



Location

Bhagsu is a village near McLeod Ganj in Dharamshala which is in Kangra district of Himachal Pradesh.
