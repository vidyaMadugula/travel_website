NATURE

Bhagsu

It is perhaps the most famous tourist spot in Dharamshala and attracts tourists who wish to revel in the glories of nature.

Markha

Set in the arid cold desert of Ladakh, Markha is a stunning river valley in Hemis National Park. The trek through Markha Valley is very picturesque and full of adventure.

Dudhsagar

Dudhsagar means the Sea of Milk owes its name to a myth. This was the favourite place of a beautiful princess who used to have a bath in a lake near the Mandovi River every day.

ATHIRAPPILLY

As you walk down the stone slabs that lead to the base of the Athirappilly waterfalls, a mysterious serenity overcomes you. It is Kerala's most famous and largest waterfall at over 80 ft high.
